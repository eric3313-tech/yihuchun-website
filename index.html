Where Great Tea Begins

A Pristine Alpine Ecosystem

Guluyunling 鈥?The "Whispering Clouds" Tea Garden

Nestled at 1,300 meters above sea level in the Miao ethnic region of Guizhou Province, our Guluyunling tea estate spans 333 hectares of pristine mountain terrain. What was once barren karst wasteland has been lovingly transformed into a National 3A-level scenic tea park, where biodiversity thrives and tea plants flourish in perfect harmony with nature.

No Glyphosate Guizhou was China's first province to legally ban glyphosate in tea gardens 鈥?our tea is truly clean from root to cup.
Biodiversity Haven Wildflowers, beneficial insects, and native wildlife coexist with our tea plants 鈥?nature does the pest control.
High Altitude Advantage Cool mountain air, abundant mist, and dramatic day-night temperature swings produce tea with exceptional aroma and complexity.
