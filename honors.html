EU Organic Certification

The European Union Organic certification is the world's most stringent organic standard. It verifies that our entire production chain 鈥?from soil preparation and seedling cultivation to harvesting, processing, and packaging 鈥?contains zero synthetic pesticides, zero chemical fertilizers, and zero GMOs. Every batch is audited by accredited third-party certifiers and is fully traceable to our Guizhou mountain garden.

Glyphosate-Free Tea Garden

Certified

Guizhou Province was the first in China to legally ban glyphosate in all tea gardens, and our plantation has never used chemical herbicides. "Better weeds than glyphosate" is more than a slogan 鈥?it is law. Independent lab tests consistently confirm undetectable levels of glyphosate residues in our tea, a critical advantage for the European market where MRL standards are tightening.

Provincial Law Guizhou Tea Industry Development Regulation (2021)
Lab Results Glyphosate: Non-detectable (LOD 0.01 mg/kg)
International Recognition

Global Tea Competition Awards

12th International Famous Tea Competition 鈥?Gold Medal

Gold Medal

The only black tea from Jiangsu Province to win Gold. Organized by the World Tea Union and hosted by the Danish Tea Culture Association, the International Famous Tea Competition is the most authoritative global tea evaluation event. Judges from China, Japan, Korea, USA, and India evaluate entries based on appearance, liquor color, aroma, taste, sweetness-return, and endurance across multiple steepings. Our "Detian Yanhong" black tea scored highest among hundreds of black tea entries worldwide.
